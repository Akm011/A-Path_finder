A* search is one of the best and popular technique used to find path and graph traversal.

In this project we consider the heuristic distance to be the eulerian path( i.e min distance between two nodes)

min heap or priority queue is used here.

IN THE PROJECT
first click --> starting node orange in color
second click --> ending node blue in color
then furthur click will form the barrier
after that press SPACE button to start the search 
then press C button to reset the grid
